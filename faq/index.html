FAQ

What is the role of clientOrderId?

The clientOrderId is a unique ID that the bot provides with every order and signal. It allows the bot creator to uniquely identify/track/monitor orders and signals, even if the BNF generated ID is not available.
It is important to note that every order needs a unique ID. If an order is added with a clientOrderId that has already been used, the order will not be accepted by BNF.

Why are there no funds in my bot?

We've received several reports that the balances of the BNF bots are empty upon creation. This is because we need to provide funds to the BNF bots, before they can start paper trading. This will happen automatically for new bots in the near future, but until that moment, please reach out to the bot creators team after creating a bot, and we'll make sure your bot is ready to go!
